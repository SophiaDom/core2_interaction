This number may appear large, but with personal relationships,
colleagues,
pupils,
friends,
neighbors,
professors,
bosses,
actresses,
musicians,
influencers,

The lifestyle you consume rightfully reflects in your brain's recognition.

PHACES serves as a documentation of faces that I wish not to forget regardless of the connection I had to them. The faces are displayed in a stylisitic way reflective of continual line portraits and street art to disguise and allude to the neglection of ifentity through memory and time.
